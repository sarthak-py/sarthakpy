Who I Am

About

I am a Chemical Engineering graduate from UIET, CSJM University Kanpur, currently based in Metro Manila, Philippines, where I work as Junior Technical Lead at Alcom Carbon Markets, a company advancing biochar-based carbon removal across South and Southeast Asia.

My work spans the full lifecycle of carbon projects: facility commissioning, technical documentation, audit readiness, and cross-office systems rollout. I spearheaded the commissioning of the Mindoro biochar facility and enabled a successful Climeworks audit.

Before Alcom, I worked with the USAID Water-Energy-Food (WE4F) Nexus regional hub at Tetra Tech, managing a 450-plus-application open-call grant program and regional conferences from Goa to Bali. Prior to that, two and a half years at IIT Kanpur supporting course development, research grants, and outreach programs that reached thousands of learners globally.

I care about climate action, systems thinking, and building things that scale.
